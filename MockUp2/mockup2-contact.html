Common Questions

Your Questions, Answered with Care

Is the consultation really free, with no obligation?
Absolutely. The first consultation is completely free. We want to understand your family and your hopes before you commit to anything. There is never any pressure.
Can I register on behalf of a family member who is in another city?
Yes, definitely. Many registrations are made by parents or siblings. We work across cities and can connect your family member with a local counsellor in their area.
How long before we hear back after submitting the form?
Always within 24 hours — typically much sooner. A counsellor will call you personally, not send an automated message.
Do you support families looking for remarriage?
Yes — with the greatest care and sensitivity. Many of our most treasured success stories involve second marriages, and we approach each one with full dignity, understanding, and no judgement.
Is a home visit truly available at no extra cost?
Yes, always. Home visits are a standard part of our service for families who prefer it — included in every package at no additional charge.
