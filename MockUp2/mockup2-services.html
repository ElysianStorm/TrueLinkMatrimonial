01 Our Core Service

Personal Matchmaking

At True Link Vivah, every match begins with a human conversation — not a click or a filter. Your dedicated counsellor listens to your full story before recommending a single profile, ensuring each suggestion is thoughtfully aligned with your values, traditions, and hopes.

We take the time to understand not just what you are looking for, but who you are — so every match feels right, not just on paper, but in your heart.

02 Family Support

Family Counselling
& Mediation

A marriage brings two families together — and our counsellors are skilled at making that coming-together a warm, joyful experience. We facilitate respectful conversations that bridge differences and help both families find shared ground.

Sessions can take place in our centre, over the phone, or in the comfort of your home — wherever your family is most at ease.

What's Included

  • Individual sessions with each family separately
  • Joint facilitated meeting between families
  • Expectation alignment and dialogue mediation
  • Support for complex situations (remarriage, inter-community)
  • Available in-centre, by phone, or as home visit
