Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the initial consultation really free? +
Yes, completely. The first consultation with a counsellor carries no cost and no obligation. We simply want to understand your family and how we can help.
How long does the matchmaking process typically take? +
This varies greatly by family. Some families find the right match within 2–3 months; others take longer — and that is perfectly normal. We never rush the process. Our role is to support you at your pace.
Can I register for a family member who is in another city? +
Absolutely. Many of our registrations are made by parents or siblings on behalf of a family member. We have centres in 15 cities and can work across locations.
Is my information kept private? +
Your privacy is our solemn promise. Your details, photographs, and family information are never shared with anyone without your explicit, specific permission at every step.
Do you also support remarriage cases? +
Yes, and we handle them with the greatest sensitivity and care. Many of our most cherished success stories involve second marriages — and we approach them with full dignity and understanding.
