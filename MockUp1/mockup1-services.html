Services Designed Around
Your Family's Needs

Every service we offer is rooted in one principle: your family deserves a trusted companion on this journey, not a transaction.

01 Core Service

Personal Matchmaking

Unlike digital platforms that rely on algorithms, every match at Ananya Matrimony begins with a human conversation. Your dedicated counsellor learns your story — your values, your family background, your traditions, and your hopes — before suggesting a single profile.

Matches are hand-selected, reviewed for compatibility at multiple levels, and presented with full context so your family can make an informed and confident decision.

Request a Consultation

What This Service Includes

  • One-on-one consultation with a dedicated counsellor
  • Detailed compatibility assessment across values, background and temperament
  • Hand-curated profile selection — no algorithm, only human expertise
  • Guided introduction and meeting facilitation
  • Ongoing support through every stage of the process
  • Regular follow-up from your counsellor after each introduction
02 Family Support

Family Counselling

Matrimony is not just a union of two individuals — it is the coming together of two families. Our family counsellors are trained to facilitate warm, respectful dialogues that align expectations, bridge differences, and ensure both families feel heard and valued.

Sessions are available in-office, by phone, or in the comfort of your home — whichever setting your family feels most comfortable in.

What This Service Includes

  • Separate initial sessions with each family
  • Joint facilitated meeting between prospective families
  • Expectation alignment and sensitive dialogue mediation
  • Support for families navigating complex situations (remarriage, inter-community, etc.)
  • Available in office, by phone, or as a home visit
03 Tradition & Astrology

Horoscope & Kundali Matching

For families for whom horoscope compatibility is an essential part of the matrimonial process, our in-house certified Jyotish experts provide detailed, traditional Kundali analysis.

Every compatibility report is prepared personally and explained clearly to families — we take the time to walk you through the findings and answer every question with patience.

What This Service Includes

  • Full Kundali chart preparation by certified Jyotish experts
  • 36-point Guna Milan assessment
  • Dosha analysis and remedial guidance where applicable
  • Detailed written compatibility report provided to the family
  • Personal consultation to discuss findings
04 Convenience & Care

Home Visit Programme

We understand that not every family can visit a centre easily — particularly elder family members or those with health constraints. Our Home Visit Programme brings our counselling service directly to your home, at a time of your choosing.

All the warmth, expertise, and support of our office consultations — delivered to your living room.

What This Service Includes

  • In-home consultation at your preferred time
  • Full counselling session with a senior counsellor
  • Profile review and discussion in the privacy of your home
  • Available across all our service cities
  • Included at no additional cost within service packages
05 Preparation & Guidance

Pre-Marriage Counselling

A match is only the beginning. Our pre-marriage counselling sessions help couples and their families prepare for the beautiful — and sometimes challenging — adjustments that come with beginning a shared life.

Sessions are conducted by trained counsellors and cover communication, family dynamics, expectations, and practical life planning.

What This Service Includes

  • Individual sessions for the prospective couple
  • Joint sessions for both families together
  • Communication and conflict resolution frameworks
  • Practical guidance on shared life planning
  • Ongoing availability of your counsellor post-wedding
