Our Legacy

A Foundation Built
by Generations

Some responsibilities choose you before you choose them. The foundation of what I built was formed long before I consciously decided to build it.

My grandfather began this work after retiring from his role as a government employee. What started as a small effort soon became a respected responsibility — bringing families together through careful understanding and sincere introductions. His approach was entirely personal: detailed records, direct conversations with families, home visits, and clarity in every alliance. To him, marriage was a serious commitment requiring thoughtful responsibility.

I grew up observing this process closely. Listening to conversations, watching the patience involved, and understanding the sincerity behind every introduction shaped my belief that connecting two individuals means shaping their future.

As the world became digital, I realized that while technology offers convenience, genuine compatibility still requires human understanding. That realization led me to carry forward his legacy through a refined, digitally integrated approach — while preserving the traditional values that built its foundation.

TrueLink Matrimony combines personal involvement with modern systems. We use technology for reach and efficiency, yet we continue to rely on manual review, personal verification, and direct interaction — because trust cannot be automated.

Our Journey

Four Decades of Milestones

1985
Founded in Chennai
Smt. Ananya Krishnaswami opens our first counselling centre with a mission to bring dignity to matchmaking.
1994
Expanding to Bangalore & Hyderabad
Demand from families across South India led to our first branch network, adding two new full counselling centres.
2003
10,000 Couples Milestone
We celebrated our 10,000th successful union — a testament to the trust families placed in our counsellors.
2010
Home Visit Programme Launched
Recognising the needs of elder families and those with mobility constraints, we introduced our in-home consultation service.
2019
National Recognition Award
Awarded Best Matrimonial Service (South India) by the National Family Welfare Council for our ethical practices.
2024
50,000 Happy Unions
Our 50,000th successful match — and our commitment to every family that walks through our doors remains as strong as ever.
